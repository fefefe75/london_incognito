INFORMATION ABOUT THE HOTEL

All rooms have a flat-screen TV. Using the in-room iPad or the free app, you can control your room's lighting, blackout blinds, curtains, temperature, and TV and radio channels. You can also enjoy with a free Disney+ accounts. Some rooms have views of Tower Bridge or the city's rooftops. The Margot Hotel offers free WiFi, a rain shower and a mini-fridge (with free bottled water). An outdoor pool is also available with life guard (put swimmers) from 10am to 6pm. There is also a restaurant open from 7:30 am to 10:30 pm allowing you to taste the gourmet food of our continent (2 Michelin stars restaurant).
